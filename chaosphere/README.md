# ⚔️ Chaosphere

Welcome to the **Chaosphere** world!   
This world is Pvp/PvE so watch out for hunters. Explore carefully! 👁️⚡

---

## 📜 About Chaosphere
- A dangerous and magical world, full of mysterious dungeons  
- Known for rare loot and unique dungeons    

---

## 🗺️ Dungeons
Here are the dungeons currently mapped in Chaosphere:

- 🟣 [Enderia](https://github.com/Vlkodlak148/Gatecraft-Coords/blob/main/chaosphere/Enderia.md) – Dungeon with Endermen and treasure
- ☠️ [Skeledar](https://github.com/Vlkodlak148/Gatecraft-Coords/blob/main/chaosphere/Skeledar.md) – Dungeon and home of skeletons
- 🧙‍♀️ [Marshes of Oblivion](https://github.com/Vlkodlak148/Gatecraft-Coords/blob/main/chaosphere/marshes-of-oblivion.md) – Swamp where witches live and brew potions
- 🐷 [Chaos Maze](https://github.com/Vlkodlak148/Gatecraft-Coords/blob/main/chaosphere/chaos-maze.md) – Confusing maze filled with pigmen and XP bottle loot 
- 🪨 [Pale Cave](https://github.com/Vlkodlak148/Gatecraft-Coords/blob/main/chaosphere/pale-cave.md) – Cave with Vex and pale oak trees 
- *(Future dungeons will be added here)*

## 🏘️ Towns
Here are the currently mapped towns in Chaosphere:

- 🏚️ [Withador](https://github.com/Vlkodlak148/Gatecraft-Coords/blob/main/chaosphere/Withador.md) – Haunted town near Skeledar, home to Wither Skeletons
- 🌋 [Netherris](https://github.com/Vlkodlak148/Gatecraft-Coords/blob/main/chaosphere/Netherris.md) – Mountain town connected to Inferno, expect lava and dangerous mobs
- 🏰 [Yorkshire](https://github.com/Vlkodlak148/Gatecraft-Coords/blob/main/chaosphere/Yorkshire.md) – Fortified town with walls, market stalls, and a port
